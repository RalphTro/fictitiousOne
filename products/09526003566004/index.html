A bar with a backstory

We work directly with two cocoa cooperatives — one in Ghana, one in Madagascar — paying above-market prices for beans that are dried on raised beds and shipped whole. The rest happens quietly in our workshop.

🌱

Single-origin cocoa

Traceable to farm level. Every batch is labelled with its harvest window and origin lot.

🤝

Fairly traded

We pay a documented premium above the fair-trade minimum, straight to the cooperative.

📄

Plastic-free packaging
